About me

Hi, I'm Tim — your piano teacher.

I'm a piano instructor with eight years of experience teaching students of all ages. With over a decade of formal classical training, I'm passionate about helping my students build a strong foundation in technique and theory.

As a versatile musician, I've released two piano albums and played keyboard for my church worship band for over 15 years. My teaching style is fun and relaxed — I tailor each lesson to your individual goals, whether you want to play classical pieces, pop songs, or even write your own music.
